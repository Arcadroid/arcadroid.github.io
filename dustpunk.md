In the year 2250, a massive solar storm known as the “Pulse of Ra” wiped out Earth’s electrical grids and boiled the oceans away. Civilization collapsed, and the world was left as a burning wasteland.

In Egypt, the ancient pyramids reacted to the storm’s energy, awakening the Eternal Pharaohs buried deep within them. They did not return as men, but as powerful techno-organic beings that now rule the desert.

Their curse spreads across the wastelands. Hordes of biomechanical warriors patrol the lost lands, destroying anyone who dares cross their territory. Every battle leaves behind fragments of the mysterious “Essence of Ra,” a strange energy capable of fusing scrap metal with mystical power.

In this shattered world, crossing the desert is more than a journey.

It’s a fight for survival against ancient gods who refuse to be defied.

Rax is just a poor drifter who wants nothing more than to roam the Lost Lands in peace. But the desert has other plans. Wherever he goes, he runs into endless hordes of enemies standing in his way.

Soon enough, he grows tired of running.

Driven by the promise of the mysterious Essence of Ra, a powerful energy that can turn scrap into deadly upgrades, Rax begins to fight back. What starts as survival may lead him to something much bigger.

If he keeps pushing forward, he might even face the Pharaohs themselves.
