Hello World!

You might be wondering what Dust Punk is, and why we're so excited for you to try it.

Dust Punk is a PvE video game set in the year 23XX in a post-apocalyptic world. The population is highly segregated among different gangs, and you, in your muscle car, will face various creatures and monsters determined to destroy you. It's your job to get rid of them and clean up the area.

We'd love to show you the full game right now, but we'll start somewhere. Here's some concept art and images of the current version of the game.
